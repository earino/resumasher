# Data Analyst — Deloitte Consulting (Vienna)

Deloitte's Vienna practice is hiring a Data Analyst to join the Analytics & Cognitive team.

## About the Role

You will work directly with clients across financial services, manufacturing, and retail to translate business questions into analytical solutions. You will partner with senior consultants on engagements that combine descriptive analytics, predictive modelling, and dashboarding.

## Responsibilities

- Build and maintain SQL-based data pipelines that feed client-facing analyses.
- Prototype machine learning models (classification, forecasting, anomaly detection) and evaluate them against client KPIs.
- Create executive-ready dashboards in Tableau or Power BI.
- Contribute to proposal writing and participate in client workshops.

## Requirements

- MSc degree in Business Analytics, Computer Science, Statistics, or a related quantitative field.
- 1+ years of hands-on experience with Python or R for data analysis (internship experience counts).
- Strong SQL skills; comfortable writing joins, window functions, and CTEs against large datasets.
- Experience with at least one visualization tool (Tableau, Power BI, or comparable).
- Fluent English; German a plus for the Vienna office.
- European work authorization.

## Nice to Have

- Prior exposure to big data tools (Spark, Hadoop) or cloud data platforms (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift).
- Consulting or client-facing experience.
- Familiarity with ML deployment (Docker, Flask, REST APIs).

## About Deloitte

Deloitte is one of the Big Four professional services firms, with a growing AI advisory practice. The Vienna office partners with clients across DACH to deliver analytics and technology engagements.
